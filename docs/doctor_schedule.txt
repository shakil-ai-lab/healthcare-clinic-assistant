8. HOLIDAYS AND CLOSURES
8.1 The clinic is closed on all designated national public holidays. A revised holiday
    schedule is posted at the front desk and on the patient portal each year in December.
8.2 Individual physician schedules are subject to change due to leave, training, or
    emergencies. Patients should confirm appointment times through the patient portal
    or front desk before traveling to the clinic.

9. SPECIALIST AVAILABILITY NOTE
9.1 Specialist physicians see patients strictly by appointment. Availability listed above

    actual appointment slots depend on current bookings.

10. SCHEDULE CHANGE REQUESTS
10.1 Requests to see a specific doctor outside their listed hours are not accommodated.
     Patients preferring a different schedule may be offered an alternate physician within
     the same department when available.
